Watching simple ingredients transform into beautiful artisan bread still amazes me every day. Every batch is crafted with patience because we believe great bread simply can't be rushed.

Whether you're stopping by for your favorite loaf of sourdough, a dozen bagels, English muffins, or our sourdough browned butter chocolate chip cookies, I'm grateful for every customer who supports our family's dream.

Baking

All doughs are mixed from scratch in small batches. Sourdough ferments on its own schedule, often 24 to 96 hours, before it is shaped and baked. Production is planned around quality and availability, not volume.

Ingredients

We use straightforward, recognizable ingredients: flour, water, salt, starter, butter, eggs, and seasonal additions when they make sense. Full ingredient lists are available on each product page in our shop.

Our Bread

Why Choose Sourdough?

At Sweet Earth Delights, our sourdough is naturally fermented and handcrafted in small batches using simple, high quality ingredients.

Benefits of Long-Fermented Sourdough

  • Easier to digest than many conventional breads
  • Long fermentation helps break down some gluten proteins, making sourdough a better option for many people with gluten sensitivity. Not suitable for those with celiac disease or a true wheat allergy.
  • May support gut health through beneficial fermentation byproducts
  • May have a lower glycemic impact than many commercially produced breads
  • Helps improve mineral availability by reducing phytic acid
  • Develops deeper flavor and texture naturally through slow fermentation

Most of our doughs ferment for 24 to 96 hours before baking, creating the flavor, texture, and quality sourdough is known for.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about ordering sourdough, pickup in Bellevue, and finding us around the Omaha metro.

How do I place an order?

Use the order form on our Contact page. Tell us what you want, how many, and your preferred pickup date. We will confirm availability and payment by email before your bake is scheduled.

Where do I pick up my order?

Most orders are picked up from our home kitchen in Bellevue, Nebraska. We will send the address and a pickup window once your order is confirmed. You can also meet us at a farmers market or pop up if that fits your schedule better.

Do you deliver?

We do not run a regular delivery service. Pickup in Bellevue is the standard option for home kitchen orders. Wholesale customers in the Omaha metro area can ask about delivery when they get in touch about partnering.

How far in advance should I order?

Sourdough and larger orders usually need a few days notice so the dough can ferment properly. Bagels and pastries often need at least 48 hours. For market weekends or holidays, earlier is better. When in doubt, send a message and we will tell you what is realistic.

Where can I find ingredient lists and allergens?

Every item on our Shop page links to a product page with a full ingredient list and allergen note. We bake in a home kitchen that also handles wheat, eggs, dairy, and tree nuts, so please reach out if you have a serious allergy question before ordering.

Where can I find you in person?

Check our delivery and market schedule for weekly retail drops and farmers market days, or use Find Us to search retail partners around the Omaha metro area.

Do you sell wholesale?

Yes. We work with a small number of cafés, shops, and market vendors in the Omaha area. Visit our Wholesale page for details on minimums, lead times, and how to start a conversation.
