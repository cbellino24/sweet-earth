Our Story

Our Home Bakery in Bellevue

Sweet Earth Delights grew out of a kitchen in Bellevue, one loaf and one market morning at a time. Today we bake sourdough, bagels, and pastries for neighbors across the Omaha metro area.

How Sweet Earth Delights Started

Most home bakeries begin the same way. Someone asks for a loaf for the weekend. A friend needs cookies for a school event. You try bagels on a Saturday morning because you have the time. People liked what they got, told someone else, and the kitchen slowly stopped being just for your own family.

That is how we got here. Sweet Earth Delights still runs out of our home in Bellevue, Nebraska. We bake in small batches so bread and pastries leave the oven fresh. Nothing waits on a shelf for days before someone picks it up.

You will find us at farmers markets and pop ups around Omaha, Papillion, and the surrounding area. A few local shops stock our sourdough too. The operation is bigger than when we started, but the goal is the same: bake food we would actually want on our own table, use ingredients we trust, and treat every customer the way we would want to be treated at a market booth.

How We Bake

Every dough is mixed from scratch in our Bellevue kitchen. Sourdough gets the fermentation time it actually needs, even when that means holding a bake for another day. Good bread cannot be rushed, and we do not pretend otherwise.

What Goes In

Flour, butter, eggs, and whatever looks good at the market that week. The same staples we reach for when cooking for our own family. If you cannot picture an ingredient on a pantry shelf, it probably is not in our recipes.

How We Sell

Order sourdough and pastries ahead for pickup in Bellevue, meet us at a farmers market, or grab a loaf from a shop that carries us. We keep the menu focused so we can bake everything well, week after week.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about ordering sourdough, pickup in Bellevue, and finding us around the Omaha metro.

How do I place an order?

Use the order form on our Contact page. Tell us what you want, how many, and your preferred pickup date. We will confirm availability and payment by email before your bake is scheduled.

Where do I pick up my order?

Most orders are picked up from our home kitchen in Bellevue, Nebraska. We will send the address and a pickup window once your order is confirmed. You can also meet us at a farmers market or pop up if that fits your schedule better.

Do you deliver?

We do not run a regular delivery service. Pickup in Bellevue is the standard option for home kitchen orders. Wholesale customers in the Omaha metro area can ask about delivery when they get in touch about partnering.

How far in advance should I order?

Sourdough and larger orders usually need a few days notice so the dough can ferment properly. Bagels and pastries often need at least 48 hours. For market weekends or holidays, earlier is better. When in doubt, send a message and we will tell you what is realistic.

Where can I find ingredient lists and allergens?

Every item on our Shop page links to a product page with a full ingredient list and allergen note. We bake in a home kitchen that also handles wheat, eggs, dairy, and tree nuts, so please reach out if you have a serious allergy question before ordering.

Where can I find you in person?

Check our 2026 schedule for farmers market and pop up dates, or use Find Us to see retail partners that stock our bread around the Omaha metro area.

Do you sell wholesale?

Yes. We work with a small number of cafés, shops, and market vendors in the Omaha area. Visit our Wholesale page for details on minimums, lead times, and how to start a conversation.
