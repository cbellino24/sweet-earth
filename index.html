Sweet Earth Delights

Find Us

How We Reach You

Sweet Earth Delights is a home-based bakery that comes to you. Through farmers markets, seasonal pop-ups, scheduled pickup from our kitchen, and select local retail partners, we share small-batch sourdough, pastries, and treats baked fresh from scratch.

Summer Farmers Markets

When market season arrives, we bring a rotating selection of sourdough loaves, bagels, pastries, cookies, and seasonal favorites — all baked fresh for the day and ready to take home.

Pop-Ups & Events

We set up at community events, holiday markets, and special bake days throughout the year. Pop-ups are a great way to try limited-run items and see what's new from our home kitchen.

Local Retail

Select shops and markets across the Omaha metro area carry Sweet Earth Delights bread on their shelves. Grab a fresh loaf from a retail partner near you.

For dates, times, and locations, visit our 2026 schedule page, find a retail partner near you, or reach out with questions.
