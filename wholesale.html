Wholesale Bread for Omaha Metro Businesses

Sweet Earth Delights supplies restaurants, retail stores, bodegas, cafés, market vendors, and neighborhood shops across Omaha and Council Bluffs. We bake small batch sourdough and pastries from our licensed home kitchen in Bellevue and deliver fresh on a schedule that fits your business.

Wholesale Bread From Our Bellevue Kitchen

Most of our wholesale accounts started with a simple question. Could we bring extra loaves to a shop? Could a restaurant get sourdough that still tasted good at dinner service? Could a market vendor get backup inventory for a busy weekend? We kept saying yes, and that is how this side of the bakery grew.

We bake everything in our licensed home kitchen in Bellevue. Shop owners, chefs, and vendors across the Omaha metro get the same bread we sell at farmers markets. It is mixed, shaped, and baked in small batches, then delivered fresh with clear labels and simple handling notes for your team.

If you are stocking a shelf, writing a brunch menu, filling a pastry case, or setting up a market table, we will figure out quantities and delivery days based on how you actually sell. We would rather have a real conversation about what works than hand you a generic price list.

  • Licensed home kitchen in Bellevue, Nebraska
  • Delivery across the Omaha metro for retail, restaurants, and events
  • Weekly standing orders or seasonal bakes

Who We Wholesale To

If you feed people for a living, or sell food to people who do, we would like to hear from you. These are the Omaha area businesses we work with most often.

Grocery & Specialty Retail

Independent grocers, natural food stores, and specialty markets looking for local sourdough from a Bellevue baker. Loaves arrive labeled and dated, ready for the bread aisle or a grab and go cooler.

Bodegas & Corner Stores

Neighborhood shops and convenience stores that want a dependable weekly loaf or pastry drop without big warehouse minimums. We size orders for smaller stores.

Restaurants & Bistros

Dinner service bread, burger buns, sandwich loaves, and shareable formats for tables. Tell us your menu and we'll suggest a bake schedule that holds up through service.

Cafés & Coffee Shops

Morning bagels, toast bread, scones, and pastries that move at the counter. Early delivery windows so your case is full when doors open.

Market Vendors & Pop Ups

Farmers market tables, holiday markets, and event vendors who need extra inventory baked to order, or a special run for a packed weekend.

Catering, Offices & Events

Corporate breakfasts, wedding platters, gift boxes, and single event orders. We plan ahead so larger quantities still come out of the oven, not off a freezer truck.

Food You Can Stand Behind

Long fermentation, scratch ingredients, and small batches. Whether the bread lands on a shelf, a plate, or a market table, your customers can taste the difference.

Delivery That Fits Your Week

Standing orders for shops, early drops for cafés, and planned runs for restaurants before service. You know what's coming and when.

Built Around Your Business

Custom quantities, seasonal features, and bakes built around your menu. We would rather grow with a few good partners than overload the kitchen.
