Wholesale Sourdough Bread in Omaha from Sweet Earth Delights

Our wholesale program grew from recurring orders with local shops, restaurants, and market vendors who needed dependable sourdough on a set schedule. What started as occasional extra loaves has become standing accounts across the Omaha metro.

Everything is baked in our licensed home kitchen in Bellevue. Retailers, chefs, and vendors receive the same products we sell at farmers markets—mixed, shaped, and baked in small batches, then delivered fresh with clear labels and handling notes for your team.

We work with each account to set order quantities and delivery days based on your sales volume and operation. Contact us to discuss product selection, pricing, and a schedule that fits your business.

  • Licensed home kitchen in Bellevue, Nebraska
  • Delivery across the Omaha metro for retail, restaurants, and events
  • Weekly standing orders or seasonal bakes

Who We Wholesale To

We supply restaurants, retail stores, cafés, market vendors, and neighborhood shops across the Omaha metro. These are the business types we work with most often.

Grocery & Specialty Retail

Independent grocers, natural food stores, and specialty markets. Loaves arrive labeled and dated, ready for the bread aisle or grab-and-go cooler.

Bodegas & Corner Stores

Neighborhood shops that want a dependable weekly drop without warehouse minimums. Orders are sized for smaller stores.

Restaurants & Bistros

Dinner bread, burger buns, sandwich loaves, and table formats. We align the bake schedule with your service hours.

Cafés & Coffee Shops

Morning bagels, toast bread, scones, and counter pastries—with early delivery so your case is stocked at opening.

Market Vendors & Pop Ups

Farmers market tables, holiday markets, and event vendors needing extra inventory or a dedicated run for a busy weekend.

Catering, Offices & Events

Corporate breakfasts, wedding platters, gift boxes, and one-time orders—planned ahead and baked fresh, not pulled from frozen stock.

Consistent Quality

Long fermentation and small-batch production. The same standards behind our market table apply to every wholesale account.

Reliable Delivery

Standing orders for retail, early drops for cafés, and planned runs for restaurants. You know what is coming and when.

Account-Based Planning

Quantities, seasonal items, and product selection tailored to how your business actually sells—not a one-size-fits-all catalog.
